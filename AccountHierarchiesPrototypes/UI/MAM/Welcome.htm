Searching for an Account — You can search for an account using search criteria, such as subscriber name, specific invoice number, address, phone number, or company name. You can also use the MAM's "Advanced Search" feature to narrow your search by selecting multiple search criteria.

Adding a New Account — You can add a new subscriber account to the MetraTech Account Manager system, however; the subscriber must be using a Service Provider that is part of the MetraTech billing system.

Viewing and Editing Contact and Account Summary Information — You can view a subscriber's contact and account information to answer subscriber's basic account questions, such as: "When is my next bill due?" You can also edit a subscriber's account information, such as their address and billing cycle.

Viewing Your Interactive Online Bill — CSRs and subscribers have the ability to view the interactive online bill at the same time.

Viewing and Editing Payment Methods — CSRs can add Automated Clearing House (ACH) accounts and add or remove credit cards and purchase cards to subscriber accounts.

Adding, Viewing, and Editing Subscriptions — CSRs can add subscriptions to accounts.

Viewing Rates for an Account — CSRs can view service charge (priceable item) rates and discount rates that apply to an account.

Setting Personal Rates — CSRs can assign personal (customized) rates to subscription components.

Viewing the Account Log and the Application Audit Log — The Application Audit log records various transactions to the MetraTech Account Manager system. It differs from the Account log which lets you view a particular subscriber's calling history with Customer Care.

Issuing Credits — CSRs can issue credits of any amount and can also approve credit requests from other CSRs. CSR access levels may vary at your site.

Specifying Additional Charges— The CSR can enter additional subscriber charges, such as a translation fee for a conference call.

Sending E-mail Notification — Subscribers can receive emails notifying them of their new user name and password or receive emails about any approved or denied credits.

Editing Aliases — You can edit aliases, and add and remove aliases to the MetraTech Account Manager. Aliases are important because they ensure proper account billing by retrieving the correct billing data for MetraTech subscriber accounts from external systems.

Adding and Updating a CSR — CSR Managers can add new CSRs to the system or update existing CSR information.
