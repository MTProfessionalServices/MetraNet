MAM

Statement of Work:
The main purpose of the following prototypes is to figure out how account hierarchies will work within MAM. The work flow for creating and updating accounts within a hierarchy along with hierarchy manipulation is covered. Complex ideas such as tree view caching and showing large hierarchies were looked into. The ability to look up accounts and work with multiple accounts at the same time was also considered.
