Restricting an Account Type from a Subscription

Problem

You want to omit an account or group of accounts from a product offering.

Solution

Use the Subscription Restrictions tab to specify which accounts are not eligible for a product offering.

Technique

  1. Click Add Account Type Restriction to open the Select Account Types window.
  2. Select one or more account types from the list.
  3. Click OK

This product offering will not be subscribable by members of the account type(s) you selected.
