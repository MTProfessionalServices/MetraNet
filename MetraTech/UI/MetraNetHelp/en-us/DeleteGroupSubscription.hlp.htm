Delete a Group Subscription

AccountSubscriptions Group Subscriptions

If usage exists for a group subscription, it cannot be deleted. You can cancel the group subscription instead.

If a group subscription is used in an account template and is deleted, it is removed from the account template.

To delete a group subscription:

  1. In the row of the group subscription you want to delete, select the Delete icon . A confirmation window appears.
  2. Click OK.
