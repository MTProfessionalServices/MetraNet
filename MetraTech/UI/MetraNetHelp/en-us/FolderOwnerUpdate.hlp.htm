Account Administrator

Account Menu BarRelationships Account Administrator

Use this page to assign or change the account administrator for the active account. An account administrator has permission to see usage and cost information for the active account, including viewing reports.

  • Click Find to open the Select Account window to search for the administrator account.

Removing an Account Administrator

  1. With the account administrator as the active account, select Relationships » Administrated Accounts.
  2. Click Delete next to the account you want to remove from administration.
