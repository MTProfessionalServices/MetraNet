About Price List Chaining

When more than one pricing model is set up for a priceable item and a particular customer, MetraNet seeks the appropriate rate in the following order:

Custom rates » Subscription Model Rates » Non-Subscription Model Rates » Default Rates

In other words, custom rates override all other rates, and subscription model rates override non-subscription model rates. This process is called price list chaining.

It is suggested that even if you have all of your customers signed up with the subscription model, that you also have a full set of non-subscription rates. This ensures that for any given usage, a charge is applied, and errors are not generated.
