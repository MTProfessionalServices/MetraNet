Add Group Subscription to Template

This page lists all the group subscriptions that have already been set up in MetraCare. Check the box next to any group subscription you wish to add to the template. You can add as many group subscriptions as you want.

The product offerings available for subscription by a particular selected account, may depend on its account type. (These account type-specific settings are configured in MetraOffer.)

If you have many group subscriptions in your system, use the Navigation Bar near the top of the page to find a specific group subscription.
