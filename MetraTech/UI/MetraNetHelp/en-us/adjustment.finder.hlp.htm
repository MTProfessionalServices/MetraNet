This page lets you find transactions for the active account according to role (payer or subscriber), period (billing interval or fixed date), and priceable item (or product offering). Then you can make adjustments to the transactions you find.

Finding Transactions to Adjust

  1. On the Transaction Finder page, specify if the active account is a payer or a subscriber.
  2. Specify the period when the transaction occurred.
  3. Select a priceable item from the drop-down list.
  4. Optionally, specify one or more properties of the transaction - such as service level, company name, or reference ID - that appear when you selected the priceable item
  5. Click OK to view all transactions that meet your search criteria.
