Account Menu BarSecurity Generate Password

When a subscriber forgets his or her password, you can have the system generate a new, temporary one that is emailed to the subscriber. To protect the subscriber's security, you do not see either the old or new password.

Before generating a password, make sure that account for which you intend to generate the new password is the active account.

The account must have a valid email address in order to have a new password generated and sent to it.
  1. Click Generate New Password.

    You are notified if the password was generated and mailed successfully.

  2. Click OK to return to the Dashboard.

    The subscriber can now log on to the system, access the interactive bill, and change the temporary password.
