MetraCare™ — Web-based customer self-care.

In today's fast-moving, highly competitive environment, meeting dynamic customer needs is paramount. As services are developed, deployed, and upgraded at record speed, market leaders must ensure that every customer experience is exceptional.

MetraCare is MetraTech's online customer care solution that helps you provide excellent customer service and increase customer satisfaction while reducing costs and streamlining operations.

Increase Customer Satisfaction
MetraCare's easy online access promotes customer self-care and improves satisfaction by allowing customers to control their own customer service experience. With MetraCare, customers can perform a broad range of account maintenance activities themselves, without having to make a call or send an email. If customers do call or email, MetraCare provides your support professionals with the comprehensive tools they need to address customer inquiries quickly and easily.
