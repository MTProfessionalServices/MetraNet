Charge In
If you charge in Advance, subscriber's charge comes due at the beginning of the billing cycle; in Arrears, the charge comes due at the end of the billing cycle.
Prorate On Activation/Deactivation
If the subscriber signs on in the middle of the first/last billing period, the system will generate a partial charge for the period that the subscriber was actually subscribed for.
Prorate Based On
Actual means that proration is based on the actual number of days in the period. Fixed means that proration is based on a fixed number of days (e.g. 30 days in a monthly period.)
