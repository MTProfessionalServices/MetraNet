[IT]Charge In
[IT]If you charge in Advance, subscriber's charge comes due at the beginning of the billing cycle; in Arrears, the charge comes due at the end of the billing cycle.
[IT]Prorate On Activation/Deactivation
[IT]If the subscriber signs on in the middle of the first/last billing period, the system will generate a partial charge for the period that the subscriber was actually subscribed for.
[IT]Prorate Based On
[IT]Actual means that proration is based on the actual number of days in the period. Fixed means that proration is based on a fixed number of days (e.g. 30 days in a monthly period.)
