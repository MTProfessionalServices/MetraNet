About Approvals

Approvals are an optional feature that requires some user changes to MetraNet web application components to be "signed off" by a second user. Your system may or may not be configured to require approvals for some types of changes.

This feature can reduce human error and, potentially, fraud in your system.

The standard components and actions that can require approvals are:

Accounts

Updating account and account view properties
