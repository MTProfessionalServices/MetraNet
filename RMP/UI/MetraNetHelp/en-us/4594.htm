Managing Bill Processing

Billing Management is a set of operational tasks related to processing usage data. Subscribers' bills are calculated by running a group of business process adapters (BPAs) at the end of subscribers' billing cycles. Additionally, account states are updated as necessary during bill processing. All processes are scheduled and executed on one or more dedicated billing servers that execute the adapters and other maintenance processes.
