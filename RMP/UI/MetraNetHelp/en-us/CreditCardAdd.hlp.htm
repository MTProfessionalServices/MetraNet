Add Credit Card

Account Menu Bar Account Details Payment Methods Add Credit Card

Enter all the required information (all bold fields) to add a new charge card to the active account.

  1. Select a Priority.

    The payment priority determines the order in which the account's payment methods will be used. The charge method with a priority of 1 will be used first. If the account already has charge methods defined, you can change this number to reflect the customer's payment preferences.

  2. Enter the credit card information:
    • Select the Credit Card Type
    • Enter the Card Number
    • Enter the CVN Number.
    • Enter the Expiration Date
  3. Click OK to add the payment method to the account.

    The payment method appears on the Payment Methods page.
