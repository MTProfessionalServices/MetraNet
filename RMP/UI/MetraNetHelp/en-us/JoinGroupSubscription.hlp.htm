Join an Existing Group Subscription

Account Menu BarSubscriptions Group Subscriptions

A subscriber account can join a group subscription only if the group subscription exists for their corporate hierarchy.

  1. Open a user account.
  2. Select Subscriptions » Group Subscriptions.
  3. Click Join Group Subscription to see a list of available product offerings available for the user.
  4. Select a product offering and click OK. The Join Group Subscription page appears.
  5. Verify the user Account Name and select a date for Effective Start Date and Effective End Date.

    If you leave the Effective End Date blank, the subscription is open-ended.

  6. Click OK to subscribe the account.
