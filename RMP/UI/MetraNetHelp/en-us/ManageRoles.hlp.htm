MetraCareSecurity Manage Roles

From this page, you can add and remove roles, and you can also add and remove the capabilities for each role.

  • You can view and edit capabilities by clicking Capabilities next to the role you wish to edit.
    See View (or Edit) Capabilities for Role.
  • You can add a new roles by clicking Add Role.
    See Adding a Role.
  • You can edit a role description by clicking next to the role you want to edit.
    See Editing a Role.
  • You can manage membership of a role by clicking next to the role you want to work with.
    See View Role Members.
