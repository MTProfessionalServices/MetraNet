Manage Hidden Product Offerings

Hidden Offerings

This screen will allow you to view the that are hidden from regular MetraOffer use. You can hide a product offering by using the Hide button in the product offering's properties screen. The hide function is useful when you want to remove unwanted product offerings from the main list in Product Offerings.

  • You can find a product offering in this list using the function located on the toolbar.
  • To restore a product offering to the main list in Product Offerings, click the Unhide button next to the product offering you want to restore.
