Viewing a Payment Method

The Payment Methods page allows you to define how you want to pay your bill. You can choose to pay with a credit card or use ACH, an electronic funds transfer system using your bank account.

ACH stands for Account Clearing House and is used to process payments through your bank from your checking or savings account. To add a payment method using ACH, you need to know your bank's routing number and your checking or savings account number.
