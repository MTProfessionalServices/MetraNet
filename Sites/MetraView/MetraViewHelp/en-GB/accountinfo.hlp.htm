Account Info

The Account Info page lets you enter and update basic account information.

This page may not be visible in some cases.

To set up or edit your account information:

  1. In MetraView, select the Account Info tab.

    Or select Bill & Payments » Edit Account.

  2. Edit the information you want to change. Bold fields are required.
  3. Click OK.
