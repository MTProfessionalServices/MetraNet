Selecting a Managed Online Bill Account

If you are a Bill Manager for other accounts, you may have permission to view their bills in MetraView. In this case, when you log in you will see a list of one or more managed bills account names.

To view your own account:

  • Select the Home tab or another tab.

To view a managed bill account:

  1. Select the Home tab.
  2. Choose an account from the Select Bill list.

    Not sure which account you are viewing?

    Always check the displayed account name.

If you make changes to others accounts, your user information is saved in the audit logs. For example, if you are editing Karen's account, the audit information will record "Admin Assistant logged in as Karen."

To log in as yourself or to view another managed bill account, you must log out first.
