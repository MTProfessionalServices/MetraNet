Working with Accounts

The Account Menu Bar appears above the Dashboard area whenever you select an account using Find an Account, Advanced Find or Account Finder, or the Dashboard's Recent Accounts. All the Account menu bar commands are related to the active account.

The sections and commands you see in the account menu bar will entirely depend on the role you perform with MetraCare and how MetraCare is configured for your organization.

Account Details

Account Summary
View a summary of a selected account's data.

Account State
Change a selected account's account state.

Update Account
Change a subscriber's account data.

Update Contacts
Change an account's contact info.

View Online Bill
View the selected subscriber account's interactive bill.

Edit Template
View or edit an account template for the selected department or corporate account.

Bill Message Associations
Lets you assign or remove bill messages from accounts.

Move Account
Move an account to a different organizational parent such as department.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions
View, create, or modify, or end subscriptions.

Group Subscriptions
View, create, or modify, or end group subscriptions.

Relationships

Account Administrator
View, add, and remove accounts administrated by the active account.

Administrated Accounts
Manage administrated accounts.

Account Owners
Change a subscriber's account data.

Pay for Accounts
Adds accounts for payment by the active account.

Edit Payers
View the payment history for the selected account or modify who presently pays for the account.

Send E-Mail
Send email to the selected account.

View Account Log
View activity for the selected subscriber account.

Add Log Entry
View MetraCare-related system events, such as issuing adjustments, creating accounts, or changing user capabilities.

Bill Manager
Manage special subscriber accounts that are responsible for other subscriber bills.

Managed Online Bills
Manage subscriber accounts whose bills are managed by bill managers.

Adjustments and Charges

Adjust Transactions
Perform adjustments on selected accounts.

Bulk Adjustments
Adjust many transactions at once.

Misc. Adjustments
Apply adjustments directly to the account (not to any particular transaction).

Nonstandard Charges
Issue nonstandard charges to an account.

Manage Billsoft Exemptions
Specify tax exemptions to an account hierarchy or to a particular account.

Manage Billsoft Overrides
Specify override tax rates and set particular taxes as exemptable.

Security

Edit Aliases
Edit, add, and remove screen aliases.

Default Security
Security permissions for new accounts.

Manage Security
Security permissions for the active account.

Generate Password
Generate new passwords for the selected subscriber account.

Lock / Unlock Account
Prevent or permit account access.

Payments

Payment Methods
Add, edit, and delete credit card and ACH payment methods.
