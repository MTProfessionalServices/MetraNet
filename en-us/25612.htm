How Approvals Work

  1. When you make a change that requires approval, the system alerts you to this fact and tells you the change is pending.

  2. Other users with the right capabilities are alerted to pending approvals:

  3. All pending changes are shown on the View Pending Changes page, where a user, or users, must approve or deny the change.

  4. When the change is approved, the change is effective immediately.
