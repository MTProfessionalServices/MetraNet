Step 4: Schedule the Report Instance

You have full control over scheduling each report instance.

In production settings, avoid scheduling report generation during peak-load times. When data reporting involves large data sets and data-intensive queries it is advisable to schedule such report generation for low-load times during your organizations daily, weekly, and monthly billing cycles.
  1. From Manage Report Instances, click Manage Report Instance Schedule for a report instance.
  2. Click Add Daily Schedule, Add Weekly Schedule, or Add Monthly Schedule.
  3. Enter the scheduling details for your report.

Reports are executed as scheduled in the format and location you have specified for the report instance.

When defining new report instances, you will usually want to test the result by queuing an ad hoc report.
