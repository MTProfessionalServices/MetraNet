Create Adjustments

Once you locate the specific transaction you wish to adjust on the Found Transactions page, you can use the various Adjustment Icons to review the current status of each Parent transaction and any associated Children transactions for the Account.  

You can then click on the specific Adjustment Icon for the adjustment task you wish to do (subject to the Adjustment Business Rules).

Only Pre Bill Adjustments may be made against rated sessions in open intervals.  

Only Post Bill Adjustments may be made against rated sessions in hard-closed intervals. Rated sessions in soft-closed intervals may not be adjusted at all.

To create an adjustment for one of the displayed transactions, do one of the following tasks:

 Adjust Parent

Click the _icon. See detailed steps.

 Adjust Children

Click the _ icon. See detailed steps.

 Reassign

Click the _ icon. See detailed steps.

 Manage Adjustments

Detailed steps for managing adjusted transactions.

If you must adjust the Parent and all Children transactions, you must do so in separate steps.
